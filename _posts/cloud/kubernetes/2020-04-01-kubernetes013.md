### 1. 탬플릿 작성

- nodeport.yaml

```yaml
apiVersion: v1
kind: Service
metadata:
  name: loadbalancer-service
spec:
  type: LoadBalancer         ------ 1. 서비스 유형
  selector:
    app: nginx-for-svc       ------ 2. 매핑될 파드 .labels 값
  ports:
  - protocol: TCP
    port: 80
    targetPort: 80
```
